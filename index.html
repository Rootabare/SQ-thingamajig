1. Who Is This Little Yellow Dragon?
Name & Meaning His name is Nailong (奶龙), which translates to “Milk Dragon” in Chinese.
Nailong is a small, round, yellow dragon with a big head and tiny limbs. He has a cute, childlike face with large eyes and a wide smile. His design is simple yet endearing, making him instantly likable. Nailong’s personality is playful and mischievous, often getting into humorous situations that showcase his innocence and curiosity. It’s such an adorable and unexpected combo, right?

Origin Story & Creator Nailong was created by Seventh Impression Culture & Media, a company based in Shenzhen, China. Designed by Lu Siying and conceptualized by Xie Cheng’en, he officially debuted on January 22, 2020, through short-form animation on Douyin (China’s TikTok) Dailymotion Unlike most mascots that begin with backstory and development, Nailong was originally designed to be a viral star—cute, round, and expressive—with no deep origin story needed. Later on, his adventures were expanded through short videos, often showing him in sci-fi escapades with a boy named Xiao Qi .

2. Chinese Mascot to Meme Icon
2024–2025: Merchandise & Spread By 2024, Nailong was being sold as plush toys, featured in theme parks, and used in brand collaborations across China and Southeast Asia You’ve probably seen him in stores like Miniso, where he’s promoted as the newest adorable mascot in their plush lineup

Rise as a Meme
On Douyin, one popular meme format showed two Nailongs facing off, declaring: "我是奶龙" (“I am Nailong”), complete with dramatic expressions and danceable sounds. These clips were perfect for remixes and spread like wildfire online Dailymotion
Global TikTok Explosion (Mid-2025) Nailong hit Western TikTok around late July 2025. A video by @angelbanglebagel amassed 3 million views in just a week, and another by @itsme_hiiyes featuring Nailong sticker GIFs reached 9 million views Gucci Bucket Hat Meme By August, Nailong was the star of the “Gucci Bucket Hat” meme trend—where characters don bucket hats and dance along to the track “Gucci Bucket Hat” by Pap Chanel x Future. People loved seeing this cute squishy dino donning high-fashion vibes—and it exploded with views within days
